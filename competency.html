Competency-Based Education

Ignis Academy students earn credit by demonstrating true mastery of a subject—no busywork, no grade inflation. Our flexible schedule allows each student to learn at their own pace and receive meaningful, personalized support.

What Makes This Different?

  • Students only earn credit by showing they’ve mastered the material.
  • Demonstrations are evaluated by a panel of teachers and community members.
  • Students can progress as quickly or slowly as needed—no waiting for the term to end.
